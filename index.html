Our Story

Handcrafted Crochet,
Straight From the Heart

TwistedThread was born out of a deep love for the art of crochet and a desire to share that passion with the world. Based in Nepal, we are a small handmade business dedicated to creating beautiful, meaningful crochet pieces that bring joy to everyday life.

From charming little keyrings to stunning floral bouquets and wearable fashion, each product is carefully crafted by hand using quality yarn and lots of love. We believe that handmade isn't just a product — it's a feeling, a connection between the maker and the receiver.
